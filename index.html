iTask

Web-based calendar & task management app

A minimal, artistic scheduling tool that blends events and to-dos in one view. Designed around flexibility — users describe what they need in natural language and the app structures it automatically. Built for people who care about aesthetics as much as function.

  • AI input parsing
  • Warm minimal aesthetic
  • Event + To-Do fusion view
  • Personalizable interface
Onboarding & Authentication — a clean entry flow with splash screen, goal-setting intro, and login / sign-up screens using Google, Apple, and Facebook SSO.

Stride

Mobile habit tracking & social motivation app

Stride is a team project from SI 582 Interaction Design at the University of Michigan. Our design question: how might we help users stay motivated and build lasting habits? We focused on three strategies — simplicity through AI-assisted input, a unified calendar-habit view, and social accountability through a real-time community feed.

  • Habit tracking
  • Social motivation
  • Figma hi-fi prototype
  • Team design project
